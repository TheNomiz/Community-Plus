About Us

Here at Community Plus, we believe in stronger communities being safer communities.
With community awareness being a principal belief of ours, we have developed our app
to put you and us at the centre of bringing about change
- for the people and places you love and want to keep safe.



Our mission is to make the world a safer place

We aim to provide real-time alerts on all crime, local and nationwide, to help people
stay safe in their communities. Not just us, but you are also a vital part of everything!
Anyone is able to report a crime or any suspicious activity in their local area,
and inform, enquire and discuss with other users at the touch of your fingertips.
